## Example 2: The Subtle Scam (Medium/High Fraud Risk)
*This example tries to sound professional but slips up by using a free email provider, missing company density, and using slightly spammy keywords like "cash bonus" which the ML will catch.*

## Example 3: The Authentic Professional Job (Safe Listing)
*This example uses a strong vocabulary (high Flesch-Kincaid readability), professional non-urgent phrasing, a solid corporate email format, and high company profile density.*

**Title**: Senior DevOps Engineer
**Company Info**: 
CloudSphere Technologies is a Series-B funded startup specializing in AI-driven distributed infrastructure. We have over 200 employees globally and are backed by leading venture capital firms. We aim to revolutionize cloud orchestration.
**Description**:
We are currently seeking a highly skilled Senior DevOps Engineer to join our core infrastructure team. In this role, you will be responsible for designing, deploying, and maintaining highly scalable CI/CD pipelines. You will collaborate closely with our backend engineering team to ensure rapid and secure code delivery across our Kubernetes clusters. We offer comprehensive healthcare, dental, and a standard 401k match. Please apply through our official corporate portal at careers@cloudsphere.com.
**Requirements**:
- 5+ years of experience with AWS or GCP.
- Deep expertise in Kubernetes and Docker containerization.
- Proficiency in Python or Go.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
